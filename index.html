FAQ

What Is Limit?

Limit refers to the maximum number of times or the amount of a particular activity or service that you can use within a given period. For example, it could be the number of games you can play or actions you can take in the app or bot.

What Is Game Limit?

Game Limit is a specific restriction on the number of games you can play or access within the app or bot. It helps to control the use of premium features or prevent abuse.

What Is Balance?

Balance is the amount of credits or points you have in your account. You earn balance through activities like playing games, leveling up, or completing tasks. It is used to purchase additional limits or game limits.

How to Get Balance?

You can obtain balance by playing games, leveling up, or participating in special events within the app or bot. Each activity can contribute to increasing your balance.

How to Buy Limit and Game Limit?

To buy limit or game limit, access the purchase menu within the app or bot. Select the amount of limit or game limit you wish to buy, confirm your purchase, and make sure you have enough balance. Your balance will be deducted accordingly.
